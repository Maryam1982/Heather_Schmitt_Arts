Beauty is everywhere. There is beauty in the physical world, in something as arbitrary as a cup of coffee or as magnanimous as the ocean. There is beauty in emotions, as jarring as heartbreak, or fluid with determination. Every atom in our existence has a beautiful element and it sometimes feels overwhelming and crowded to process such. So for me, I choose to paint it and release the tension of all of that amazing beauty trapped behind a creative mind that sees it, but sometimes can’t quite get out the words.”-
