Heather Schmitt is a southern California based artist working with acrylic on canvas. Self taught with a favoritism toward large scale pieces, Heather has been creating works since she could hold a brush. Often caught drawing on legs during class, walls while talking on the phone and always carrying a pen to draw out her thoughts, she has truly grown up expressing who she is through the tools of the artist. She enjoys losing herself within the canvas and embraces raw emotion in hopes of spilling out into the piece.
